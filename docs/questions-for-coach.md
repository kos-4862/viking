# Questions For Coach

## 1. Club basics

- Full club name: `SC Viking` or another official version?
- Short description of the club in 1-2 sentences?
- Year founded?
- City and training locations?
- Main age groups?

## 2. Contacts

- Main phone number for parents?
- WhatsApp number for communication?
- Telegram contact if it should be public?
- Email address for the site?
- Exact address or addresses for training?
- Google Maps links if available?

## 3. Social links

- Confirm Facebook: `https://www.facebook.com/sc.viking`
- Confirm Instagram: `https://www.instagram.com/fcvb.2021/`
- Confirm TikTok: `https://www.tiktok.com/@fcvb.2021`
- Any YouTube channel?

## 4. Hero block

- Main headline for the homepage?
- Short subheadline under the main title?
- What should be the main call to action:
  - `Book a trial`
  - `Contact coach`
  - `Join the team`
- Do we have a main hero photo or video?

## 5. Programs

- What age groups do you actually train?
- Are there beginner and advanced groups?
- Are there separate groups for girls?
- Are there individual training options?
- Do you run camps or seasonal intensives?

For each program:

- Program name
- Age
- Schedule
- Location
- Price if public
- Short description

## 6. Schedule

- Training days and times for each group?
- Indoor / outdoor?
- Do schedules change by season?
- Do you want the full schedule public on the site?

## 7. Coaches

- Names of coaches?
- Roles of each coach?
- Short bio for each?
- Licenses / achievements?
- Photos available?

## 8. Training philosophy

- What is the main coaching philosophy?
- What makes SC Viking different from other clubs?
- What do parents usually value most?
- What 3-4 ideas should the homepage emphasize?

## 9. Enrollment flow

- What happens after a parent leaves a request?
- Who responds first?
- How fast do you usually reply?
- Should the site promise a trial session?
- Should applications go to Telegram, WhatsApp, phone, or all together?

## 10. Prices

- Do you want prices public?
- Monthly fee?
- Trial session free or paid?
- Discounts for siblings or packages?

## 11. Media

- Do we have:
  - logo in PNG or SVG
  - coaches photos
  - training photos
  - match photos
  - short promo video

## 12. Legal / trust

- Do you need privacy policy?
- Do you need consent text for form submissions?
- Are there partner logos or sponsors?

## 13. Site priorities

- What is more important:
  - more trial requests
  - more WhatsApp messages
  - more calls
- Which pages are needed first after homepage:
  - Programs
  - Coaches
  - Schedule
  - Locations
  - Contacts

## 14. Telegram setup later

When ready, ask the coach for:

- Telegram notification destination:
  - personal chat
  - group chat
- Bot token from BotFather
- Chat ID

## Short version if coach has little time

- Official club name
- Phone
- WhatsApp
- Address
- Age groups
- Schedule
- Coach names
- 1 short club description
- 5-10 best photos
